# Etiology
- Fetal anomalies
	- Urethral obstruction (e.g., posterior urethral valves)
	- Bilateral renal agenesis
- Maternal conditions
	- Placental insufficiency
	- Late or postterm pregnancies (> 42 weeks of gestation)
	- Premature rupture of membranes

# Diagnostics
