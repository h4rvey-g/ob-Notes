# Causes of tender hepatomegaly
## Acute hepatitis
- Infection (eg, [[hepatitis A]] virus, hepatitis E virus, bacterial, parasitic)
- Drug-induced liver injury
- [[Alcoholic hepatitis]]
## Impaired venous outflow
- Budd-Chiari syndrome
- Right heart failure
