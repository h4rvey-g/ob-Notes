# Etiology
- Systemic causes
	- Sepsis (most common cause), e.g., secondary to trauma, infection or peritonitis
	- Trauma
	- Shock
	- Massive [[transfusion]] 
	- Acute pancreatitis
- Primary damage to the lungs
	- [[Pneumonia]]
	- Aspiration
	- Inhaled toxins
	- Drowning incidents

>[!tip] 
>Sepsis is the most common cause of ARDS.
